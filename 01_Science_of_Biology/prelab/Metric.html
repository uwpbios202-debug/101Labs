MCAT-Relevant Examples

Cell Biology

A typical eukaryotic cell is about 10-100 micrometers (μm) in diameter.

10 μm = 10 × 10⁻⁶ m = 1 × 10⁻⁵ m

Biochemistry

DNA base pairs are approximately 0.34 nanometers (nm) apart.

0.34 nm = 0.34 × 10⁻⁹ m = 3.4 × 10⁻¹⁰ m

Pharmacology

Drug concentrations are often measured in milligrams per milliliter (mg/mL).

5 mg/mL = 5 × 10⁻³ g / 10⁻³ L = 5 g/L

Microscopy

Light microscopes can resolve objects as small as 200 nanometers (nm).

Electron microscopes can resolve objects smaller than 1 nanometer (nm).
