Key Concepts:
  • Cell Wall: Rigid outer boundary in plant, bacteria, and fungi cells
  • Cell Membrane (Plasma Membrane): Thin flexible barrier in all cells
  • Selectively Permeable: Controls what enters and leaves the cell
  • Homeostasis: Maintaining stable internal conditions for survival
  • Intracellular Homeostasis: Stable internal environment within cells
  • Animal Cells: Have cell membranes but never have cell walls
  • Cell Wall Functions: Provides support, shape, and protection
  • Membrane Function: Acts as gatekeeper for cellular equilibrium
