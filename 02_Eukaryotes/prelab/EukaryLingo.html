How This System Works

This interactive system will help you become fluent in biological terminology through a proven 6-step process:

  1. Select a term from the dropdown menu
  2. Copy the AI prompt and paste it into an AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, or Copilot)
  3. Explain the morpheme and etymology in your own words
  4. Apply the term with examples from your lab manual
  5. Listen to correct pronunciation and practice aloud
  6. Assess your pronunciation using Microsoft's Speech Assessment Tool
